#Business Requirement Document
#Version 2.0

Taxes for employees are calculated like this:

1) For full time employees

- Fixed $100 amount for base health insurance
- 16% income tax
- 10% retirement tax

2) For part time employees

- Fixed $100 amount for base health insurance
- 16% income tax
- 5% retirement tax

3) For interns
- If income < 350 => no tax due to state aid
- Else => only 16% income tax
